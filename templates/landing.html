FEATURES

Built for real-world documents

Most translation tools choke on scans. Inkshift was built from the ground up for them.

Smart OCR

PaddleOCR reads faded, skewed, and low-resolution scans with high accuracy. Angle correction built in.

Layout Preserved

Text is erased and redrawn at the exact original position. Tables, columns, and multi-page PDFs all handled correctly.

Numbers & Codes Untouched

Dates, invoice numbers, product codes, and serial numbers are detected and skipped — only natural language is translated.
